Croquet Game Variations

Discover the diverse world of croquet through these engaging variations of the classic game. Each variation offers unique challenges and excitement for players of all skill levels.

Golf Croquet

The most popular social version of croquet, known for its simplicity and quick gameplay.

  • All players progress through the same hoop sequence
  • First to score 7 hoops wins
  • Simple turn sequence and scoring
  • Perfect for beginners and social play

Association Croquet

The traditional competitive format, offering complex strategy and precise skill development.

  • 26-point game with advanced tactics
  • Break play and strategic positioning
  • Complex rules and scoring system
  • Tournament standard format

Snake Croquet

A free-flowing variant that emphasizes continuous play and tactical decisions.

  • Continuous gameplay through a snake pattern
  • Focuses on positioning and strategy
  • Popular for club events
  • Great for developing tactical skills

One-Ball Association

An introduction to Association Croquet principles using simplified equipment.

  • Uses Association rules with one ball per player
  • Focus on break play fundamentals
  • Ideal transition to full Association
  • Popular training format

Gateball

A Japanese mallet team sport inspired by croquet, featuring fast-paced strategic gameplay.

  • Played by two teams (red and white) of up to five players
  • Score points by hitting balls through gates and the goal pole
  • 30-minute games with strategic team play
  • Popular worldwide with international competitions
  • Watch Gateball tutorial videos
