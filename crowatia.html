Crows play Crowquet with Crowbars in Crowatia at the Russell Crowe Invitational

Played in the woods near Lake Varna the Russell Crowe Invitational took place on a secluded forest court under variable conditions. The setting rewarded patience, accuracy, and an ability to adapt as light and footing changed throughout the day.

The final between Crow Kay and Crow Bar proved decisive and absorbing. Crow Bar built an early lead through assertive break play, while Crow Kay responded with disciplined positioning and a series of well-judged recoveries. Momentum shifted several times before Crow Bar ultimately converted late opportunities to secure the match. Crow Magnon and the Rook from Zagreb were the losing semifinalists.
