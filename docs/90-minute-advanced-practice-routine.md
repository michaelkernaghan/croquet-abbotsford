# 90-Minute Advanced Croquet Practice Routine

## Warm-Up (10 min)
- **5 min:** Straight roquets from 5 yards - 10 shots
- **5 min:** Hoop running from 1 yard, dead center - 10 hoops

---

## Block 1: Single-Ball Precision (20 min)
*Wylie: "Reliable roquets and hoop strokes can compensate for weaker croquet strokes"*

- **10 min:** 10-yard roquets - 15 attempts, track percentage
- **5 min:** 2-yard angled hoops - 5 left, 5 right
- **5 min:** Uncontrolled hoop runs - run hard, find ball after (builds confidence)

---

## Block 2: Break Building (25 min)
*From MacRobertson 2022 analysis*

- **10 min:** Four-ball break - hoops 1-6
  - Focus on pioneer placement before each hoop
  - Stop shot vs roll shot decisions

- **10 min:** Three-ball break recovery
  - Start with one ball out of position
  - Practice creating new pioneer positions

- **5 min:** Rush control - dolly rushes to specific targets

---

## Block 3: Peeling Practice (20 min)
*From Wylie's Triple Peel article*

- **7 min:** Jawsing practice - place ball in hoop jaws from various angles
- **7 min:** 4-back peel attempts - straight peel, then rush-peel
- **6 min:** Irish peel attempts - both balls through together

---

## Block 4: Leaves & Wiring (10 min)

- **5 min:** NSL (New Standard Leave) setup - practice ball placement
- **5 min:** Wiring - create effective wires using hoop 1 or 2

---

## Cool-Down: Pressure Shots (5 min)

- **5 shots from B-baulk at ball in corner 4** - long shot accuracy under simulated pressure
