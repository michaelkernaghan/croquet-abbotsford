## 200-Word Description (Website/Detailed)

Experience the elegance and enjoyment of croquet with this comprehensive introductory course designed specifically for adults and seniors. Over six weeks, you'll learn all aspects of this classic lawn game—from understanding the basic rules and court layout to mastering fundamental shots and developing winning strategies.

Croquet is an ideal activity for active adults and seniors because it provides gentle, low-impact exercise while encouraging social interaction and mental engagement. The game is played at your own pace on level grass surfaces, making it accessible to a wide range of abilities and fitness levels.

Our experienced instructors from the Abbotsford Croquet Club will guide you through hands-on learning in a supportive, fun environment. Each 90-minute session includes instruction, practice time, and opportunities for friendly games. All equipment—including mallets, balls, and hoops—is provided.

Classes are held at the scenic Mill Lake Park croquet courts, offering a beautiful outdoor setting in the heart of Abbotsford. The course culminates in a friendly tournament where you can showcase your new skills.

No experience is necessary, and modifications are available for different abilities. By course end, you'll have the confidence to play independently and may even want to join the Abbotsford Croquet Club for ongoing play. Register today and discover your new favorite pastime!

## FAQ Snippets

**Q: I've never played croquet. Is this course for me?**
A: Absolutely! This course is designed for complete beginners. No experience necessary.

**Q: Do I need to bring equipment?**
A: No, all equipment is provided including mallets, balls, and hoops.

**Q: What if it rains?**
A: We have rain dates built into the schedule. You'll be notified of any cancellations.

**Q: Is croquet difficult to learn?**
A: Not at all! The basics can be learned quickly, and our instructors make it fun and easy.

**Q: Do I need to be physically fit?**
A: Croquet is low-impact and suitable for most fitness levels. We accommodate different abilities.

**Q: Can I continue playing after the course?**
A: Yes! Course participants are welcome to join the Abbotsford Croquet Club for ongoing play.
