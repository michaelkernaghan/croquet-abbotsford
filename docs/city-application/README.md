# City of Abbotsford Recreation Course Application

Application materials and guidance for offering a public recreation course on croquet instruction for adults and seniors.

## Project Overview

**Course Title:** Croquet Instruction for Adults & Seniors
**Target Audience:** Adults and seniors in Abbotsford
**Location:** Mill Lake Park Croquet Courts
**Organization:** Abbotsford Croquet Club

## Purpose

This project contains all documentation, guidance, and materials needed to apply to the City of Abbotsford for approval to offer a public recreation course teaching croquet to adults and seniors.

- **application-draft.md** - Draft application to the city

## Application Process

1. **Preparation Phase**
   - Define course objectives and curriculum
   - Identify qualified instructors
   - Determine scheduling and capacity
   - Develop budget and pricing

2. **Application Submission**
   - Complete city recreation course application
   - Submit required documentation
   - Provide proof of insurance (if required)
   - Submit instructor qualifications

3. **Review & Approval**
   - City reviews application
   - Address any questions or requirements
   - Receive approval and course listing

4. **Implementation**
   - Finalize course schedule
   - Set up registration system
   - Promote course through city channels
   - Deliver instruction

## Key Considerations

### Course Benefits
- Promotes active lifestyle for seniors
- Low-impact physical activity
- Social engagement and community building
- Accessible sport with minimal equipment needs
- Uses existing city park facilities

### City Requirements
- Liability insurance
- Instructor qualifications/certifications
- Safety protocols
- Accessibility accommodations
- Equipment management
- Registration process

### Success Metrics
- Enrollment numbers
- Participant satisfaction
- Retention rates
- Community engagement
- Pathway to club membership
