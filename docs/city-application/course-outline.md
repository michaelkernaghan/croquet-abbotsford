## Course Description

Learn the classic lawn game of croquet in a fun, social, and low-impact environment. This course introduces participants to the fundamentals of croquet, including rules, techniques, and strategy. Perfect for adults and seniors looking for gentle outdoor exercise and social connection.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:
- Understand the basic rules and etiquette of croquet
- Demonstrate proper mallet grip and swing technique
- Execute fundamental shots (approach, rush, croquet stroke)
- Navigate a standard croquet court layout
- Participate in recreational games with confidence
- Continue playing independently or join club activities

## Weekly Session Plan

### Session 1: Introduction to Croquet
- Welcome and course overview
- History and basics of croquet
- Equipment familiarization
- Proper grip and stance
- Basic stroke practice

### Session 2: Court Layout & Rules
- Understanding the croquet court
- Game objective and sequence
- Basic rules of play
- Turn order and scoring
- Practice: Simple approach shots

### Session 3: Fundamental Shots
- The approach shot
- The roquet (hitting another ball)
- Taking croquet (positioning)
- Practice drills and exercises

### Session 4: Strategy Basics
- Shot selection
- Positioning for advantage
- Defensive vs. offensive play
- Partner play (doubles)
- Mini-games and practice

### Session 5: Game Play & Tactics
- Full game walkthrough
- Advanced shot techniques
- Common situations and solutions
- Strategy discussion
- Supervised game play

### Session 6: Review & Social Play
- Skills review and Q&A
- Tournament-style friendly games
- Continued play options
- Club membership information
- Celebration and wrap-up
