Croquet Mallets

We provide Oakley Woods croquet mallets for both use during play and purchase. Our selection includes both traditional ash mallets and modern compound designs.

Mallet Use

  • Free use during your first two trial visits
  • Free use during your first lesson
  • Seasonal rental: $20 per season
  • Purchase option: $50 for used mallets
  • All mallets sanitized before storage

Available Mallets

  • Sport - Ash wood with square head
  • Grange - Ash wood with square head
  • Extreme - Plastic compound with round head
  • Predator - Ash wood with square head

Available in 32", 34", or 36" heights
