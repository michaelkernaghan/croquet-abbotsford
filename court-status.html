Court Information

Our croquet courts at Mill Lake Park are maintained to provide an enjoyable playing experience. Regular maintenance ensures optimal playing conditions throughout the season.

Court Availability

Courts are typically available for play during our regular session times (Tuesdays and Thursdays at 5:30 PM). Courts may occasionally be closed due to maintenance, tournaments, or inclement weather.

Court Rules

  • Please wear flat-soled shoes on the courts
  • No food or beverages on the playing surface
  • Return all equipment to the storage area after use
  • Respect other players and follow croquet etiquette
  • Report any court issues to club staff
