Welcome to the 2025 Abbotsford Golf Croquet Invitational at Mill Lake Park in Abbotsford, BC. This page contains everything you need to know before tournament day – from registration details and match schedule to code of conduct and local amenities.

Registration & Entry

  • Event Date: Saturday, May 24, 2025
  • Entry Fee: $2 per player (cash on site)
  • Player Cap: 8 competitors – register early!
  • How to Register: email abbotsfordcroquet@gmail.com with the completed entry form (or request one by email).
  • Equipment: Mallets supplied if required – feel free to bring your own.

Required Information

Please be ready to supply your full name, contact details, emergency contact, mailing address, club affiliation/handicap (if any), and any pertinent medical notes. You must also sign the event waiver and code‑of‑conduct agreement.

Player Code of Conduct

We expect courteous, sportsman‑like behaviour at all times. Highlights include:

  • Respect opponents, officials, and spectators.
  • Adhere strictly to Golf Croquet rules and rulings.
  • Look after equipment and the lawn – flat‑soled shoes only.
  • No harassment, bullying, or discriminatory language – zero tolerance.
  • Arrive promptly for matches and remain ready to play.

Tournament Format & Pairings

With eight players we will run a block round‑robin followed by cross‑block knock‑out semifinals and a championship final.

  • Two blocks of four players (drawn at 09:00).
  • Each block plays a single 13‑point game against every other player in its block (3 games each).
  • Top two from each block advance: A1 vs B2 and B1 vs A2.
  • Semifinals and final will be single 13‑point games (best‑of‑3 for the final if time allows).

Time Limits & Resolving Games

All block games have a strict 30-minute time limit to ensure the tournament runs on schedule.

  • When time is called, both players complete their current turn.
  • After that, each player is entitled to one additional shot.
  • The winner is determined by the player who has scored the most hoops at that point.
  • If scores are tied after the additional shots, play continues in "sudden death" mode until one player scores the next hoop to break the tie.
  • Time limits will be enforced by a designated timekeeper.

Venue Facilities

The tournament is hosted at the Jubilee Lawn Bowling facility, Mill Lake Park. On‑site public washrooms (flush toilets) are beside the horseshoe pits. The clubhouse offers shade, limited seating, a microwave, and kettle. Parking is free at the Ware Street entrance.

Local Restaurants

Looking for food before or after play? Popular nearby spots include Duft & Co Bakehouse (breakfast/lunch pastries & coffee), Greek Islands Restaurant (hearty Greek fare), Lou's Grill (pub classics), Nikko Sushi, and The Table Bistro overlooking the park.
