Golf Croquet Tournament

Saturday, September 27, 2025

Tournament Director: Michael Kernaghan

Location: Mill Lake Lawn Bowling Courts

Event Synopsis

The Abbotsford Croquet Club invites you to attend our Golf Croquet Tournament at the Mill Lake Lawn Bowling Courts. This event features competitive singles play in a beautiful park setting.

The entry fee is $2. Registration is capped at 8 players, so don't delay if you're interested in participating!

Tournament Details

  • Entry Fee: $2 per player
  • Format: Singles competition
  • Equipment: Mallets available for those who need them
  • Venue: Mill Lake Lawn Bowling Courts

Tournament Schedule

  • Saturday (September 27):
    • 8:00 AM — Courts open for practice
    • 9:00 AM — Set up and Registration
    • 10:00 AM — Tournament Start
    • 1:00 PM — Lunch Break
    • 2:00 PM — Afternoon Matches
    • 4:30 PM — Finals and Awards Ceremony

No food or beverages will be provided. Please bring your own refreshments and lunch. Casual attire is acceptable - whites are not required.

Location

The Mill Lake Lawn Bowling Courts provide an excellent venue for croquet tournaments, with well-maintained playing surfaces and beautiful surroundings. The venue offers:

  • Professional-grade lawn surfaces
  • Convenient parking
  • Beautiful park setting
  • Accessible facilities

Address: Mill Lake Park, Abbotsford, BC

The area offers numerous attractions for visitors, including:

  • Historic Downtown Abbotsford
  • Beautiful hiking trails and parks
  • Fraser Valley wine tours
  • Various dining and shopping options

Registration Information

For more information and to register, please contact our Tournament Director at abbotsfordcroquet@gmail.com

Registration deadline: September 20, 2025
