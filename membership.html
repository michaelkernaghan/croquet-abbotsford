Join Our Croquet Community

Membership Benefits

As a croquet player at Mill Lake Park, you'll become a valued member of the Abbotsford Lawn Bowling Club, enjoying access to:

  • Professional-grade lawn facilities
  • Complete sets of croquet equipment
  • Instruction from experienced players
  • Regular playing sessions (Tuesday and Thursday evenings, 6 PM)
  • Tournament opportunities
  • Social events and community activities

Our Partnership

The Abbotsford Lawn Bowling Club has expanded its sporting offerings to include croquet, creating a vibrant community for both sports. This partnership allows us to:

  • Maintain exceptional playing surfaces
  • Share resources and facilities
  • Build a stronger sporting community
  • Offer multiple lawn sports options

The two sports complement each other perfectly, offering different styles of play and athletic engagement to suit various preferences and abilities.

Regular Playing Times

Evening Sessions

  • Every Tuesday and Thursday
  • 6:00 PM start time
  • April through September

Additional Saturday events include Open Houses and tournaments. See our calendar for details.

How to Join

We welcome players of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced competitors. Your membership includes:

  • Access to all club facilities
  • Equipment use during sessions
  • Basic instruction for newcomers
  • Tournament participation opportunities
